Health Portal

West Carleton Family Health Portal

While working for my client Nova Networks, I redesigned a health portal for the patients of West Carleton Family Health Team to use at home or on the go. I was the design lead for this project where I used an iterative approach through design and front-end development in a duration of 2 months. The goal was to change the interface to be more user-friendly for a primary target audience of 50+ with better information structure. This way they would be able to use the portal with less friction as well as making it cross-compatible.

You can visit their health portal login to view the design and interaction which I also designed and developed. However, you need to be a member with credentials in order to see the portal.
