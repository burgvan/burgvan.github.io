Health Portal

West Carleton Family Health Portal

This is a health portal re-design for desktop for West Carleton Family Health that I did while at Nova Networks. I was the design lead for this project where I used an iterative approach through design and front-end development. The goal was to change their current interface into a more usable interface with better information structure for their patients to be able to use without too much friction. The back-end development is still in the works among the development team.
