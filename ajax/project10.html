Centrum Redesign

A respsonive redesign of Centrum's website.

This is a responsive redesign of Centrum's website that I helped designed when I was working for Mirum as a freelancer. Mirum's client Pfizer wanted a brand new user experience while keeping Centrum's brand in mind, and I worked closely with the Associate User Experience Director, Stephanie Poce and designer, Corey Litvak. The goal was to retain customers and bring in potential new customers to learn about their products' benefits to their health. I mainly worked in the intial stages of the project on re-structuring the information architecture, creating wireframes, and conducting some research for the "finder tool". Along the way, I did a few revisions on a few key landing pages near the end of the project as well as writing the functional specifications for the developers.
