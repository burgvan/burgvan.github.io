Real Wool Shop Website

An e-commerce site for the Real Wool Shop.

This website was designed and developed on Shopify. We were a team of three people who had a period of 8 weeks to re-design the Real Wool Shop's website. We went through stages to reach to the finished product - research, wireframing, mock-ups, and front-end development. This included weekly meetings with the client. The website is currently live and running with some slight alterations made by the client later on but the majority of our design is still present.
