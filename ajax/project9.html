Creative Television Application

A Video on Demand TV application owned by Sony.

This is a television application owned by Sony called Crackle that embraces a whole new, creative viewing experience for their users. I was involved in the early discovery/brainstorming stages of this project within a team of two other interaction designers. We analyzed current wireframes given by a third party that needed a lot of improvement, brainstormed new ideas, researched requirements, created experience maps, new wireframes and conducted a usability testing for the main user interfaces.

The lead of this project was Matthew Ernst at You.i and this application is powered by You.i built on You.i Engine.
