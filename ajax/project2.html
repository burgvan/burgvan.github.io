WET Redesign

An open source framework called the Web Experience Toolkit.

While working at Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat, this was one of the projects where my team re-designed key landing pages on the Web Experience Toolkit (WET) website. It promotes an open source framework for public servants and the general population to use. It's considered to be usable and accessible which is why Government of Canada is now using this framework for Canada.ca. We focused on improving the user experience and information architecture of this website.

Some of my responsibilities in the team were to actively use Github for the re-design and gather feedback from the WET community. I used an iterative approach to my design where I developed and refined the design based on feedback and evaluation, and my team and I also conducted two rounds of usability testings, prior to the redesign and after for further validation of the new design.
