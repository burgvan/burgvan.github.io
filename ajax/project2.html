WET Re-design

An open source framework called the Web Experience Toolkit.

While working at Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat, this was one of my projects where I re-designed the Web Experience Toolkit (WET) website, it promotes an open source framework for public servants and the general population to use. It's considered to be usable and accessible which is why Government of Canada is now using this framework for Canada.ca. It's available on Github which I actively used for my re-design of the WET website and gather feedback from the WET community. My team and I also conducted two rounds of usability testings, prior to the re-design and after for further validation of the new design.
