Data Filtering Interface

A concept for a new user interface for the Proactive Disclosure.

This was another project I worked on during my time at Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat where I developed a new concept for the Proactive Disclosure. Its current design is not user-friendly and does not guide users with a smooth filtering approach. I focused on the visualization of the data and the search design as part of the work flow. I decided to implement a faceted search that is also somewhat guideded to not overwhelmn users with too many options. I used an iterative approach to my design where I developed and refined the design based on feedback and evaluation. I designed two versions of the interface and facilitated a short A/B participatory testing/workshop at CodeFest 2014 where I managed to have people who were attending the conference to take the time to analyse two versions of the data filtering interface. They were largely printed and posted on a wall, and people wrote comments and ideas on sticky notes, as well as voting which one they would prefer to use.

After deciding which interface would be preferred based on feedback at CodeFest and from a Github community, I made changes and documented the design which ended up getting deployed as a working prototype by a fellow developer with whom I discussed how the design works. I have also proposed the design to various stakeholders and the concept might be getting worked on for Open Data.
