Data Filtering Interface

A concept for a new user interface for the Proactive Disclosure.

This was another project I worked on during my time at Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat where I developed a new concept for the Proactive Disclosure. Its current design is not user-friendly and does not guide users with a smooth filtering approach. I consider my design to be not only a focus on the user interface but also the visualization of the data and information architecture. I documented my design and discussed with my fellow developer to deploy my design as a working prototype. I used an iterative approach to my design where I developed and refined the design based on feedback and evaluation. I have also proposed my design to various stakeholders and the concept is currently being worked on for Open Data.
