Recharge Yourself

It's time to disconnect and get some shut-eye.

I developed and designed a concept for a campaign ad that brings awareness to youth and adults on how today's technology use affects their sleeping patterns which can lead to health problems and poor work ethics. More and more people are having a hard time to disconnect themselves from technology. I believe the visuals and facts demonstrate this concern quite well.
