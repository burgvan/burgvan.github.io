About me

My name is Vanessa. For about 6 years now, I have been researching and creating solid user experiences for web, mobile, TV, and Internet of Things related platforms. Not only do I have a good eye for aesthetics but also for functionality that meets users' goals without making them think too much about the interface and how to complete a goal. I like to do most of the thinking for them. Research has been an integral part of the design process for me, which is why I know UX best practices and techniques for mobile and desktop. Aside from being a bit of a nerd, I'm also a full-time music lover, art enthusiast, and sometimes a dreamer.
