Beauty & Wellness Web App

Purpose

This was a project for the BrainStation UI Design Course that I completed in December, 2019. I felt it was a good time to cultivate my UI design skills further because I wanted to move into the direction of being involved in all stages of product development. I'm continuing to work on it as a passion project. I chose to challenge myself by designing a new website from scratch that serves a potential real-life need.

Results

I designed a web app that provides customers with a convenient way of finding beauty and wellness certified professionals online (hairstylists, make-up and nail artists, massage therapists) to have them come to customers' locations as the main focus. The secondary focus is also providing an option to go to the professional’s business location. The end goal is to provide an easy way to book a luxurious experience at the customer's location (their home or event), and for those that may have anxiety, discomfort or mobility issues to visit salons/spas.

For this course project, we had options to jump into UI design or do some UX and research in the beginning before working on the UI design. I was one of the few students that had a strong UX background, and as a true UX professional, I felt the need to start with light market research using an online survey to get an idea of what the market is like for a beauty and wellness web application, and what most customers would prefer.
